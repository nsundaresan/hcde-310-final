A Safer Home

This application provides an interactive tool that maps homes for sale in the Seattle area, and all police report incidents that have occurred in the city of Seattle in the last three months. The purpose of this project is to create a visual representation of police incidents in Seattle in relation to homes for sale that can aid potential homebuyers in their decision to purchase a Seattle home. The police report incidents is not a comprehensive representation of crime in an area, but ideally, this application will help homebuyers gain a better understanding of the current safety surrounding their future home.

INTENDED USE

While this application is useful to any Seattle resident who is curious to the crime rates surrounding their home, the intended audience is potential homebuyers who are interested in a Seattle home. Ideally, the user will visit this site with prior knowledge of the specific address to a home for sale, or a specific neighborhood of Seattle that they are interested in purchasing a home in, in order to optimize their experience.

DATA

This app utilizes two datasets. The first dataset, collected by the Seattle Police Department,contains data on reported Seattle police incidents, updated every 15 minutes. The crime data is the most recent, taken from the last three months up until today.The second dataset, collected by, Zillow contains property information for houses listed for sale, which is updated daily.'

STRUCTURE

There are two different options of how you can choose to view the data: searching by a home address, or searching by a neighborhood. In both of these options, you will be able to select and filter the kinds of police reports they wish to see on the map.
